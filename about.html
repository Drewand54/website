General

Hey there! I'm Andrew, a first-year honors student studying computer science at Arizona State University. While I'm still in the early stages of coding, I've already created some cool projects like a word search solver, a spell checker, an autonomous maze-navigating robot, and now, my own website! The ability to solve any problem and create something which is truly your own is what keeps me hooked. So far I've really enjoyed computer science and I'm excited to see how much I will learn in the coming years. When I'm not writing lines of code, you'll often see me hiking, lifting weights, listening to music, playing the piano, or as of recently, painting. These hobbies offer a creative outlet and help break up my structured school life. I'm passionate about learning and growing in the tech world, hoping to absorb all the knowledge and experiences this field has to offer. Whether it's building a solution for a small issue or working on a team to solve something greater, I believe it is vital to stay curious, be creative, and work hard. I'm always up to talk about coding, music, nature, hobbies, or anything else so feel free to contact me!
